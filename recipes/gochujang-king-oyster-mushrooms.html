Gochujang King Oyster Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 350g King Oyster Mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Gochujang sauce
  • 2 tbsp Sake
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1/2 tbsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Slice the King Oyster mushrooms lengthwise.
  2. Mix all other ingredients.
  3. Marinade the mushrooms with the sauce for 20 minutes.
  4. Cook the mushrooms until the sauce turns into a glaze.
