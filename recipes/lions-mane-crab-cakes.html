Lion's Mane Crab Cakes

Ingredients

  • Lion's Mane mushrooms
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Onion
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • Some other spice you can't find

Steps

  1. Shred the mushrooms.
  2. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients until fully incorporated.
  3. Make patties.
  4. Cook the patties for 3 minutes on each side.
